Vasaqa Fiji: Elevating Culinary Excellence to Art

Vasaqa Fiji (right before the hotel turnoff) is a BBQ lover’s dream, serving up tender ribs, juicy steaks, and pulled pork. Their Fijian fusion dishes mix traditional flavors with a modern twist, making every bite a tasty surprise. There are also plenty of mouthwatering vegetarian options for plant-based diners. And of course, you can wash it all down with a tropical cocktail made from fresh, seasonal fruit. Big portions, great prices, and unbeatable flavor—what’s not to love?

Location:

  • Wailoaloa Rd, Nadi
